Classes 9

core:Pose
A concrete pose occurrence within a salutation sequence. Central hub connecting asana, body parts, breathing, errors, variant, and sequence neighbours.
core:Asana
Named yoga posture (e.g. Pranamasana, Bhujangasana). May be equivalent to another asana via sameAsanaAs.
core:BreathingPattern
Enumerated breath phase coordinated with a pose. Three named individuals: Inhale, Exhale, Hold.
core:PostureRule
Prescriptive alignment cue for a pose — what the body should do (e.g. “keep spine elongated, gaze forward”).
core:PostureConstraint
Restrictive condition limiting how a pose may be performed (e.g. “knees stay over ankles, never beyond toes”).
core:PoseError
A known incorrect execution pattern.
core:CorrectionInstruction
Remedial cue paired with one or more errors.
core:BodyPart
Anatomical region a pose engages.
core:Variant
A named Surya Namaskar tradition — the scope to which each pose instance belongs.
